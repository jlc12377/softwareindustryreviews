Best Project Management Software — 2026 Rankings

By Editorial Team Updated March 31, 2026 10 products ranked 72,000+ reviews analyzed
Methodology-based ranking · How we rank
We evaluated 10 project management platforms using a weighted methodology: User Satisfaction (35%), Feature Depth (25%), AI & Automation (15%), Pricing Transparency (15%), and Scalability (10%). Scores are aggregated from G2, Capterra, and TrustRadius. The PM category is splitting in two directions in 2026 — AI-native features are creating a capability gap between platforms investing in autonomous agents and those still focused on traditional task management.
