Email marketing remains the highest-ROI marketing channel for small businesses, with average returns of $36 for every $1 spent according to Litmus. But choosing the right platform is harder than it looks — there are 200+ tools targeting SMBs, ranging from $0 free tiers to $1,000+ per month, and the differences between them aren't always obvious until you've used them.

We evaluated 47 email marketing platforms specifically for the small business segment. The top 10 below are ranked on user satisfaction scores across G2, Capterra, and TrustRadius, deliverability rates from independent testing services, pricing transparency, ease of use for non-technical owners, and the strength of free or starter tiers. Each ranking includes real pricing, real review counts, and clear use case recommendations.

FAQ

Common questions

What's the best free email marketing platform for small business?
For most SMBs, HubSpot's free tier is the strongest choice — 2,000 sends per month with a free CRM included is hard to beat. Mailchimp's free tier (500 contacts, 1,000 sends) is the simplest. Brevo's 300 emails per day with unlimited contacts is best for businesses with large but inactive lists. MailerLite's free tier (1,000 subscribers, 12,000 sends) is the most generous for solopreneurs.
Mailchimp vs Constant Contact — which is better for small business?
Mailchimp wins on design flexibility, integrations, and overall product depth. Constant Contact wins on ease of use, US-based phone support, and event marketing features. Mailchimp is better if you have some technical comfort. Constant Contact is better if you want to call someone for help. For pure pricing, Mailchimp's free tier beats Constant Contact's 60-day trial.
How much should a small business spend on email marketing software?
For SMBs with under 5,000 contacts, expect $15-50 per month. Once you cross 10,000 contacts, expect $50-150 per month. eCommerce businesses on Klaviyo or Omnisend typically spend more because pricing scales with contact volume — but the revenue from email usually 10-20x covers the cost. As a rule of thumb, email marketing software should cost no more than 5-10% of your total marketing budget.
Which email platform has the best deliverability?
Independent testing from EmailToolTester and similar services typically puts ActiveCampaign, MailerLite, and Klaviyo at the top for deliverability. Mailchimp and Constant Contact perform well but slightly behind. Deliverability matters more than raw price — landing in spam folders defeats the purpose, regardless of how cheap the platform is.
Do these platforms integrate with QuickBooks, Shopify, and Square?
All 10 platforms have direct Shopify integrations. For QuickBooks integration, HubSpot, Mailchimp, Constant Contact, and ActiveCampaign have native connections. Square integrates natively with Mailchimp and Constant Contact, and via Zapier with most others. Always verify the specific integration depth — some are deeper than others.
